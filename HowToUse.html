How To Use?

  1. Select the "Calculation" option in the website navigation
  2. After the calculation page appears, select the device you want to add.
  3. After selecting the device, click the "Add device" button.
  4. You can add or reduce the quantity of the device
  5. Select the electricity capacity in your house, if the electricity capacity in your is not in the option, select the "Other..." option and type down the electricity capacity of your house.
  6. Total electricity usage will appear below your devices list
  7. From the total electricity usage and electrical capacity of your home, results will emerge whether your home can handle your devices optimally or not
